About Us

The institute is affiliated to the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (formerly known as WBUT, recognised by UGC), with College Code – 340. Since its inception, the institute always focused on imparting value-based quality education. The success of our institute can be seen through our placement records as some of the leading names in the industry have been recruiting students from our institute.

University Affiliated UG Courses

BCA (Bachelor In Computer Application)

Bachelor in Computer Application (BCA) is a three-years undergraduate degree course for students who wish to develop into the world of Computer languages. The course is one of the most popular options to get started with a career in Information Technology. A degree in BCA is equivalent to the degree in Computer Science or Information Technology.

BHM (Bachelor In Hotel Management)

Bachelor in Hotel Management is a three-years full-time degree course divided into six semesters including Industrial Training for students who want to take up a career in the Hotel/Hospitality establishments especially in Food Production, Food and Beverage Service, Front Office Operations and Housekeeping Operations. The objective of the course is to train students to develop skills in Hotel Management and Hospitality Services.

BBA in Hospital Management

BBA in Hospital Management is a three-years full-time degree course divided into six semesters including Summer Training for students who want to take up the career in the Health Care sector especially in the smooth operation of Hospital as well as to ensure the proper service to the patients. The objective of the course is to train students to develop skills in Hospital & Health care Services.
